## Understanding Reimbursements

Birdflop operates as a nonprofit and returns excess profit to active clients:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Quarterly Calculation">
    Every quarter, we calculate excess profit from operations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Regional Rates">
    Reimbursement rates vary by region (US vs EU) based on operational costs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Distribution">
    Active clients receive credit within 31 days of quarter end
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Application">
    Credits are automatically applied to your account for future payments
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Active clients** are defined as users with at least one non-canceled, non-suspended service at the time of distribution.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Yes! You can change your RAM allocation or switch between plans at any time through the client panel. Changes are prorated automatically.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments through our secure payment processor.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a setup fee?">
    No setup fees! You only pay for your monthly hosting plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I'm not satisfied?">
    We offer a 3-day satisfaction guarantee. Request a full refund within 3 days if you're not happy with our service.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do reimbursement credits expire?">
    No, credits never expire and remain on your account until used. However, only external payments (not credit usage) count toward future reimbursements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
