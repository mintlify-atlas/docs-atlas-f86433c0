# Where Do My Payments Go?

Birdflop is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. As such, all profit generated is reinvested into improving our services and accomplishing our mission. Your service fees are used for covering our server costs, including building new servers, colocation fees, server rental fees, and software licensing fees.

## Quarterly Financial Report

We proudly display our quarterly financial reports to maintain transparency with our community. Our latest report shows revenue (inner ring) and expenditures (outer ring) for Q1 2025.

<Note>
Some numbers may be approximations, and categories may be simplified. US Hosting Expenses includes depreciation.
</Note>

## Revenue Allocation

Your payments are allocated to:

### Infrastructure Costs
- **Server Hardware**: Building and maintaining new physical servers
- **Colocation Fees**: Costs for housing servers in data centers
- **Server Rental**: Rental fees for additional capacity
- **Software Licensing**: Required software and tools for operations

### Service Improvements
- **Network Infrastructure**: Maintaining high-speed, reliable connections
- **Hardware Upgrades**: Keeping servers up-to-date with the latest technology
- **Monitoring Systems**: Tools to ensure 24/7 uptime and performance

### Public Resources
- **Free Tools Development**: Creating and maintaining resources like RGBirdflop
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive guides and support materials
- **Community Support**: Discord server and support infrastructure

### Client Reimbursements
- **Excess Profit Returns**: Periodic reimbursements based on excess profit
- **Price Optimization**: Keeping costs as low as possible for the community

View detailed server statistics and performance metrics on our [Node Stats](https://birdflop.com/node-stats) page on the main website.

## Our Commitment

Your payments get you the best possible rate while contributing to the development of our free public resources. We operate with the following principles:

- **Never Overload Servers**: We maintain optimal server capacity to ensure performance
- **Reinvest All Profit**: Every dollar of profit goes back into services and mission
- **Maximize Value**: We reimburse clients based on excess profit
- **Full Transparency**: Regular financial reporting to our community

## Geographic Operations

We operate infrastructure in multiple regions:

- **US Hosting**: Servers and infrastructure in the United States
- **EU Hosting**: Servers and infrastructure in Europe

Expenses and reimbursements are tracked separately by region to ensure fair pricing and accurate financial reporting.

<Note>
All financial decisions are made with our nonprofit mission in mind: providing affordable, accessible hosting while fostering a passion for technology and computer science.
</Note>
