# About Birdflop

Birdflop is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 93-2401009) dedicated to igniting and nurturing a passion for technology and computer science through affordable and accessible hosting and resources.

## Our Mission

At the heart of our mission, we are dedicated to igniting and nurturing a passion for technology and computer science. We uniquely approach our mission by offering affordable and accessible hosting resources, not just as a service, but as a catalyst for technological curiosity.

Our belief is rooted in the idea that the hands-on experience of creating and managing a game server can be a gateway to a lifelong interest in technology and computer science. By ensuring this journey is engaging and frustration-free, we significantly enhance the likelihood of sparking a deeper interest in technological fields.

<Info>
Birdflop is the only 501(c)(3) nonprofit server host, dedicated to public resources, communities, and you.
</Info>

## What We Do

Birdflop goes beyond mere hosting; we actively foster a community of learning and growth through:

- **Affordable Hosting**: We provide Minecraft server hosting at some of the industry's best rates, starting at $1.48/GB RAM after reimbursements
- **Free Public Resources**: We develop and maintain free tools and resources available on our website, including RGBirdflop and other community tools. See the [Tools section](/tools/overview) for details.
- **Community Support**: We build and support a community of learners through our Discord server and documentation
- **Client Reimbursements**: As a nonprofit, we reimburse clients based on excess profit, keeping costs as low as possible

## Nonprofit Status

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, all profit generated is reinvested into:

- Improving our services and infrastructure
- Developing free public resources
- Expanding our reach and impact
- Supporting initiatives that fuel a passion for computer science and technology

<Note>
Donations to Birdflop are 100% tax-deductible in the US. If you would like to support our mission, please consider making a charitable donation.
</Note>

## Looking Ahead

We are committed to expanding our reach, investing in initiatives that fuel a passion for computer science and technology, and making a lasting impact in shaping future innovators. Our goal is to continue providing accessible entry points into technology fields while maintaining the highest standards of service and community support.
